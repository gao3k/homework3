Historical Moments in Georgia

Georgia has a rich history filled with significant events. Here are some notable moments:

  • Founding of the Colony of Georgia by James Oglethorpe in 1733.
  • Battle of Kennesaw Mountain during the American Civil War in 1864.
  • The Civil Rights Movement, including the Atlanta Student Movement and the influence of leaders like Martin Luther King Jr.
  • Hosting the 1996 Summer Olympics in Atlanta.
